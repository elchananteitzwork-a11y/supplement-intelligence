## 1. Executive Summary

A daily supplement for women experiencing stress-related hair shedding (telogen effluvium) — positioned on the cortisol mechanism rather than the biotin deficiency narrative that dominates the market. The category proof point is extraordinary: Nutrafol (a single hair supplement brand) was valued at **$3.5 billion** by Unilever in early 2025, with ~$300–350M in annual revenue, 80% of DTC revenue from repeat purchasers, and 63% of customers ordering six or more times. The specific differentiation is the "biotin counter-narrative" — 62% of women with hair loss do NOT have a biotin deficiency (PMC data), yet biotin is the #1 purchased hair supplement globally. The product wins by naming the real mechanism (cortisol → telogen effluvium) and building a formula around it: KSM-66 ashwagandha (27.9% cortisol reduction in 60-day RCT) + iron bisglycinate (ferritin restoration; 70.3% of female alopecia is iron-driven) + supporting DHT blockers and vitamins. Subscription mechanics are the best of any analyzed category — hair loss subscribers maintain indefinitely out of fear of recurrence.

**Is this worth building? → YES**

## 7. Market Gaps (10)

1. **The biotin counter-narrative is unclaimed** — 62% of women with hair loss don't have a biotin deficiency (PMC); biotin is the #1 hair supplement; "stop taking biotin" is the most powerful entry story in all of supplements and no brand has built a brand around it
2. **The ferritin threshold gap is underdressed** — Lab "normal" ferritin is 15–30 ng/mL; hair growth requires ≥70 ng/mL; 70.3% of female alopecia is iron-driven; almost no mainstream supplement includes iron bisglycinate
3. **Cortisol-first messaging is unclaimed even though Nutrafol has ashwagandha** — containing ashwagandha ≠ leading with cortisol. Nutrafol's messaging is "whole-body systems" — the cortisol mechanism story belongs to whoever takes it
4. **The $45 price gap vs Nutrafol's $88** — a credible alternative at half the price captures a documented segment of price-sensitive buyers who know Nutrafol works
5. **Post-stress-event supplement** — telogen effluvium peaks 2–4 months after the stressor (divorce, illness, job loss, postpartum); no product is positioned for this specific, predictable moment
6. **No postpartum specific formula** — postpartum hair loss is one of the most searched, most emotionally distressing moments; no dedicated supplement brand owns it
7. **"Biotin causes false thyroid tests" awareness** — high-dose biotin interferes with thyroid blood assays; women on thyroid medication are unknowingly getting false results; a brand that addresses this safety gap builds enormous trust
8. **No maintenance dose architecture** — no supplement has a built-in "after your hair grows back, here's your lower-dose maintenance phase" — this prevents the "I don't need it anymore" churn moment
9. **Personalized ferritin-based protocol** — no brand offers a "test your ferritin, then take the right iron dose" approach despite iron being the #1 deficiency driver
10. **Male hair loss (DHT + stress)** — the biotin counter-narrative + cortisol + DHT formula works across genders; male-specific positioning is a second product with a fresh audience

## 10. Product Recommendation

### Formula
| Ingredient | Dose | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **KSM-66 Ashwagandha** (Withania somnifera, 5% withanolides) | 300mg | Primary cortisol reduction — 27.9% drop in 60-day RCT; protects hair follicles from cortisol-induced shedding |
| **Iron bisglycinate** | 25mg | Restores ferritin to ≥70 ng/mL; 70.3% of female alopecia is iron-driven; bisglycinate form = no GI side effects |
| Zinc bisglycinate | 25mg | Anti-androgenic; supports follicle health; common deficiency in stressed women |
| Saw palmetto extract (45% fatty acids) | 200mg | Natural DHT inhibition; androgenic hair loss component |
| Vitamin D3 | 2,000 IU | Low vitamin D is documented in telogen effluvium patients |
| L-Lysine | 500mg | Iron absorption co-factor; independent hair growth data |
| Methylcobalamin (B12) | 500mcg | Oxygen delivery to follicles; deficiency worsens shedding |

### Key Ingredients to AVOID
- High-dose biotin above 1,000mcg (interferes with thyroid and iron blood tests)
- Selenium above 200mcg (can cause hair loss at high doses — the opposite of the goal)
- Vitamin A above 5,000 IU (documented hair loss trigger at megadoses)
- Generic ferrous sulfate iron (causes constipation and bloating; patients stop taking it)

### Dosing: 2 capsules/day with morning food (consistent daily window important for cortisol rhythm)

### COGS: $5–9 / 30-day supply (at 5,000 unit MOQ)
