# Supplement Opportunities — 2-Problem Pairs Only
**Rule:** 1 main problem + 1 naturally co-occurring secondary problem. Maximum 2.  
**Format:** Supplements only.  
**Date:** June 2026

---

## Scoring Criteria (each 1–10)
| Criterion | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Demand** | Volume of people actively experiencing this exact pair |
| **Repeat Purchase** | Does the customer need this daily/indefinitely? |
| **Ease of Mfg** | Available ingredients, stable formula, simple to make |
| **Marketing Clarity** | Can a stranger understand the product in one sentence? |
| **Brandability** | Can you build a memorable brand around this pairing? |

**Total possible: 50 points**

## #1 — Stress + Sleep
**Main problem:** Can't sleep  
**Secondary problem:** Can't stop stressing  
**Total Score: 49 / 50**

| Criterion | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Demand | 10 | Largest cross-demographic health complaint; 70%+ of Gen Z anxious daily; insomnia epidemic |
| Repeat Purchase | 10 | Taken every night; indefinite use |
| Ease of Mfg | 9 | KSM-66 ashwagandha + magnesium glycinate = well-studied, widely available, stable |
| Marketing Clarity | 10 | "You can't sleep because you can't stop stressing. This fixes the reason, not the symptom." |
| Brandability | 10 | PM ritual, calming aesthetic, strong naming potential (Unwind, Settle, Still) |

**Core Insight:** Melatonin — the #1 sleep supplement — improves sleep onset by only 7 minutes vs. placebo. The real problem is cortisol, not melatonin deficiency. The market is running on the wrong solution.  
**Key Ingredient Stack:** KSM-66 ashwagandha (cortisol suppression) + magnesium glycinate (GABA activation)  
**Price Point:** $35–55/month  
**Customer:** Women 25–50, professionals, anyone who "can't turn off their brain at night"  
**Positioning Hook:** *"Melatonin doesn't work because you don't have a melatonin problem."*

# Top 3 Immediate Priorities

### 1. Stress + Sleep (Score: 49)
The category dominant product is provably wrong (melatonin). You don't need to create demand — you need to redirect it. A cortisol-first sleep supplement using KSM-66 + magnesium glycinate can be manufactured simply, priced at $40–50/month, and positioned with a single insight: *"Melatonin treats the clock. This treats the stress."*

### 2. Hair Loss + Stress (Score: 47)
Biotin is bought by millions of women who don't know it doesn't work for stress-related loss. The counter-positioning story is one of the strongest in supplements: *"Stop taking biotin. Start treating the actual cause."* Long retention (customers commit to 6 months minimum because hair takes time to grow).

### 3. Bloating + Fatigue (Score: 46)
Enormous demand, simple formula, daily use, and a clear pairing that customers recognize from lived experience. The insight that "gut problems don't just cause bloating — they cause fatigue" is legible without education. High-volume opportunity with accessible manufacturing.
